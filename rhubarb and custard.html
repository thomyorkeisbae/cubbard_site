Rhubard and Custard

One of Jessica's favourites, this cocktail is sweet and punchy.

Ingredients

50ml rhubarb vodka

50ml apple juice

50ml lemonade

simples!

Instructions

Measure 50ml of apple juice in a jug, then add 50ml of lemonade and 50ml rhubarb vodka to the same jug until you have 150ml of liquid.

Add ice to a medium-sized glass and pour the contents of the jug over

Enjoy! Try not to use a straw to save the turtles and other various marine life...
